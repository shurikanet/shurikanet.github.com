Cutting Edge IT Solutios for Automotive Transportation

Celebrity Box is for everybody who uses cutting edge gadgets within the vehicle. With Celebrity you can simply attach your smartphone or tablet to the vehicle wiring and acoustic and enjoy the freshiest navigation, media, cloud apps in sync with your car entertainment.

Simplicity Shell

Simplicity Shell provides you with easy and powerful graphic interface and framework for CarPC, tablet and other automotive infotainment applications. It works like a shell over existing operating systems including Windows, Linux and Android.

DSRC solution

Componentality is the World's first company providing low cost and fully functioning DSRC solution. With our Saha SDK you may apply Car to Car and Car to Infrastructure systems with minimum possible budget and maximum of effectiveness.

Multimedia and Internet terminals

We offer another value for public transportation. Componentality multimedia and Internet terminals help your passengers be online, play, listen music and watch video while their long and boring travels.

Welcome to Componentality!

You’ve made a right turn. Wherever you are, Componentality Oy helps you and your clients to stay connected and your data remain with you. After years of success, we provide complete platform solutions for CarPCs, in-vehicle connectivity, multimedia, Car-to-Car and Car-to-Infrastructure systems.
